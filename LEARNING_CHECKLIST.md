**Folder order:** Below, dated session folders are listed **oldest → newest** (ascending by date). `Assignment-*` and `Class-*` folders come last.

**Legend**

- [x] Done  
- [~] In progress / partial  
- [ ] Not started  

*(Default new items use `[ ]`. Change to `[~]` while working, then `[x]` when finished.)*

**How to track VTT conversion**

- **Converted?** — You turned the WebVTT into study notes (outline, bullets, or cleaned prose) in a separate file.
- **Confirmed?** — You personally confirm this folder’s VTT task is complete (tick when ready).
